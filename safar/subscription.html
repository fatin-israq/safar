Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our subscription plans

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be charged the prorated difference immediately.

For Basic plan users, additional rides will be charged at standard rates. Premium and VIP plans have unlimited rides included, so you never have to worry about exceeding limits.

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. After that, no refunds are provided, but you can cancel anytime to avoid future charges. Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period.

No hidden fees! The subscription price includes all mentioned benefits. Additional charges only apply for services outside your plan's scope, such as premium vehicle upgrades or airport pickup fees.

When you choose a yearly plan, you pay for 10 months and get 2 months free - that's a 17% savings! You'll be billed once annually and can still change or cancel your plan if needed.

Currently, we don't offer subscription pausing. However, you can cancel anytime and resubscribe when you're ready to start riding with us again. Your benefits will resume with your new subscription.
